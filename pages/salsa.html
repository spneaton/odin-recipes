Matty's Morning Salsa

I went through this really shitty breakup and felt like junk. So one weekend I went down to San Diego and visited one my best friends named Matt. Matty is awesome, he always finds a way to make me feel better. We had a blast golfing and doing stupod shit together. One morning he made some eggs and whipped this up. I loved it. Matty is a great cook, always has been. Try it out, add whatever you think would make it good for you.

Ingredients

  • 1 Avacado
  • Basil
  • 5-6 Cherry Tomatoes
  • Chopped Spring Onion
  • Jalapenos (optional)
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper to Taste

Steps

  1. Cut everything up.
  2. Put it in a bowl.
  3. Drizzle olive oil over it.
  4. Add some salt and pepper.
  5. Thanks Matt.
