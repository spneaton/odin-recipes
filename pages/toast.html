Ma's Cinnamon Toast

This one is funny because I think there are lot of us with a shared experience over this "recipe". It's the every mom cinnamon toast. Every now and then I'd come downstairs ready for school and for breakfast ma made our favorite simple meal, cinnamon toast! I still make it every now and then whenI want something quick and sweet. If you didn't grow up with this that's okay I'm sure you have a friend who could totally vouche for this. Thanks Mom's. This was awesome.

Ingredients

  • Toast
  • Cinammon
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Just make it by feel, do you really need measurements?

Steps

  1. Make some toast.
  2. While the toast is still hot spread butter on it and let it melt.
  3. Cover the top with a cinammon and sugar mixture.
  4. Eat it. Try it again, figure it out.
  5. Get on the bus and go to school happy you had cinnamon toast this morning... that's how it was for me as a kid at least.
